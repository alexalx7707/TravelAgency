Greek Island Hopping

By Alexandra S.

Our Greek island tour was beautiful, but had a few rough spots. The good: Elena planned a wonderful route through Santorini, Mykonos, and Naxos with stunning accommodations. The local guides were knowledgeable and engaging. The not-so-good: a ferry cancellation cost us a day, and I felt the agency could have been more proactive about finding alternatives. Would recommend, but suggest building in buffer days for island hopping.
